# StreamDrop Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: May 17, 2026

StreamDrop helps users discover streaming movie releases and save titles to a local watchlist.

## Information We Store

StreamDrop stores app preferences on your device, including selected language, streaming service, genre, release window, notification preference, and saved watchlist titles.

This MVP beta does not require an account and does not collect names, email addresses, passwords, payment details, or precise location.

## Movie Data

StreamDrop uses movie metadata, images, ratings, release dates, providers, and related information from TMDB. StreamDrop is not endorsed, certified, or otherwise approved by TMDB.

## Notifications

If enabled, StreamDrop may schedule a weekly Friday reminder on your device. You can turn this off in Settings.

## Feedback

If you send beta feedback by email, your email app may share your email address and any details you include in the message.
